Rising Athlete: Zubin Gohain from Assam, India

Zubin Gohain, a young high jumper from Lakhimpur, Assam, has rapidly risen as a promising athletic talent on the national and international stage. Born around 2009, he has already won significant accolades including a Bronze Medal at the Asian Youth Games 2025 in Bahrain and a Gold Medal at the National Junior Athletics Championship. Selected through Assam’s Khel Maharan talent program, Zubin has consistently demonstrated exceptional skill in high jump, clearing heights above 2 meters in youth categories. His achievements have earned him felicitations from the Assam Chief Minister and widespread recognition as a symbol of the state’s growing sports prowess. Zubin’s journey from grassroots initiatives to international success highlights the potential of dedicated sports development programs in Northeast India, making him an athlete to watch in coming years
